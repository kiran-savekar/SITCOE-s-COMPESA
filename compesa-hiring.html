Become a COMPESA member!

Interested in honing your organizational and interpersonal skills? Ever wondered what goes behind the scenes at a SITCOE's COMPESA? Don't wait anymore.
COMPESA brings to you an opportunity to work with our team to learn all this and more! We at COMPESA are looking for someone who is passionate about team-work, a great team player, and open to learning new skills to join our team!

Check out more details

The COMPESA-membership would see you handling variety of functions such as:

  • 1. Handling social media channels of COMPESA including Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter etc.
  • 2. Providing support in conceptualizing events.
  • 3. Working with the team on a marketing strategy.
  • 4. Assisting with social activities, events, webinars.
  • 5. Building a database for COMPESA.
  • 6. Writing articles/blog posts for the COMPESA's website.
