Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for SITCOE C4C Program ?

Kindly fill the registration form available on website with correct academic details. Then, after successful student verification, you will receive SITCOE C4C Program joining link to registered email address.

Is it Free or Paid ?

Of course! It's free for students of Sharad Institute of Technology, College of Engineering, Yadrav.

Who can apply for SITCOE C4C Program ?

Students who are currently enrolled in any stream of Sharad Institute of Technology, College of Engineering, Yadrav can apply. (We will be verifying your student status by accepting your student identity or any legal document)

When I get the certificate ?

After successful completion of any course [including quizzes / projects], certificate will be issued to you which can be accessed under Coursera My Account section.
